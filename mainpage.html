Why Tiny Tide?

Addressing the real challenges parents face

The Challenge

  • Managing daily routines of newborns is overwhelming
  • Lack of experience leads to forgotten feeding times
  • Missing vaccination schedules due to poor tracking
  • No organized system for health records
  • Stress from juggling multiple baby care tasks

Our Solution

  • Simple, intuitive app for daily routine management
  • Automated reminders for all important activities
  • Complete vaccination tracking with notifications
  • Digital health records accessible anytime
  • Peace of mind with organized baby care
